9:00 AM

Columbia Flower Market

Perfect way to spend a Sunday morning, grab a coffee and stroll through the floral markets. Listen to lively buskers and pick up some gorgeous flowers. Ideal for tourists and locals alike.

11:30 AM

Borough Market

The variety of foods is overwhelming (in a good way), the prices are very reasonable, and the quality is great. Definitely recommend trying the raclette, and oysters (as pictured)!

1:30 PM

Hyde Park

Clean, beautiful, relaxing and just generally a wonderful attraction. I've spent many a day at Hyde Park throughout my childhood and adult life. Lovely place to have a picnic or ride a bike.

4 PM

Tate Modern

Favorite museum in London - it's free, and you could spend the whole day here. Great views from the top, especially love the Louise Bourgeois exhibition.

7 PM

Dishoom

Best Indian restaurant in London, with great & lively ambiance and nostalgic decor. The chicken tikka, chicken ruby and naan are highly recommended.
